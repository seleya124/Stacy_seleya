Chapati & Beans

Coastal comfort food

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup cooked beans
  • Coconut milk
  • Spices
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare chapati dough
  2. Cook beans with coconut milk
  3. Roll and cook chapatis
  4. Serve together
