Ugali & Fish

A lakeside classic

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 whole tilapia
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Traditional spices

Instructions

  1. Prepare the fish with traditional spices
  2. Make ugali by boiling water and adding flour
  3. Cook fish with tomatoes and onions
  4. Serve hot with greens
